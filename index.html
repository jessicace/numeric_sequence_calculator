Numeric Sequence Calculator

Instructions

  • Enter a number into the text field below.
  • The calculator accepts positive, whole numbers only.

Number Sequences

  1. Natural Numbers: All numbers up to and including the number entered.
  2. Odd Numbers: All odd numbers up to and including the number entered.
  3. Even Numbers: All even numbers up to and including the number entered.
  4. All numbers up to and including the number entered, except when:
    • Output C when a number is a multiple of 3.
    • Output E when a number is a multiple of 5.
    • Output Z when a number is a multiple of both 3 and 5.
  5. Fibonacci Sequence: All fibonacci numbers up to and including the number entered.
